What game is about?
a boy/girl named John/Janet has been interested in extreme sports since childhood, actively participating in cycling and snowboarding competitions. In one of these tournaments, a “veteran” of Elthon sports asked him/her. He immediately saw the undying potential and decided to invite the youngster to the country of sports. Further adventures, podiums and triumphs depend only on you!
Now, as soon as you have been accepted into the Sport Republic, you will need to promote your career, conquer new tracks, chase records, make new acquaintances
